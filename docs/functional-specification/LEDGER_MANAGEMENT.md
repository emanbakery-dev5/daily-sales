# Ledger Management Functional Specification

**Project:** EMA Bakery Distribution Management System (EMA-BDMS)

**Module:** Ledger Management

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Approved

---

## 1. Purpose

The Ledger Management module is the financial accounting engine of the EMA Bakery Distribution Management System.

It records, maintains, and reports all customer financial transactions generated by operational activities. The ledger is the single source of truth for customer balances, sales, payments, returns, adjustments, and outstanding receivables.

Ledger records are generated automatically from business operations. Manual ledger entries are restricted to authorized financial adjustments and corrections.

All ledger transactions are immutable. Corrections are performed by posting reversing or adjustment entries rather than editing historical records.

---

## 2. Business Objectives

The Ledger Management module shall:

- Maintain customer account balances.
- Record sales generated from completed dispatches.
- Record customer payments.
- Record product returns.
- Record financial adjustments.
- Calculate outstanding balances automatically.
- Support customer account statements.
- Integrate with Dispatch Management.
- Integrate with Payment Management.
- Integrate with Reporting.
- Maintain complete audit history.

## 7. Ledger Transaction Lifecycle

```text
Business Event

↓

Ledger Entry Created

↓

Customer Balance Updated

↓

Available for Reporting

↓

Included in Statements

↓

Locked
```

Definitions

**Business Event**

A completed operational process such as a dispatch, payment, return, or adjustment.

**Ledger Entry Created**

A new immutable financial record is generated.

**Customer Balance Updated**

The customer's outstanding balance is recalculated.

**Available for Reporting**

The transaction is immediately available for financial reporting.

**Included in Statements**

The entry appears in customer account statements.

**Locked**

The record becomes permanently read-only after posting.

The Ledger Management module automatically generates ledger entries from completed business operations.

---

### Automatic Posting Workflow

```text
Business Transaction

↓

Validate Transaction

↓

Generate Ledger Reference

↓

Create Ledger Entry

↓

Update Customer Running Balance

↓

Generate Audit Event

↓

Refresh Customer Ledger

↓

```

Ledger posting occurs inside a single database transaction.

If any step fails, the entire posting operation is rolled back.

## 53. Business Rules

The Ledger Management module enforces the following rules:

- Every ledger entry has a unique Ledger Reference Number.
- Ledger references are immutable.
- Historical ledger entries cannot be edited.
- Historical ledger entries cannot be deleted.
- Corrections require adjustment entries.
- Running balances are system calculated.
- Customer balances update automatically.
- Ledger entries are permanently linked to their originating business transaction.
- All financial posting occurs inside database transactions.
- Duplicate postings are prevented.
